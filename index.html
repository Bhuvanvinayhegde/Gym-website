Popular Classes

Cardio

Cardio is the zone where you burn the most fat and calories. Some of the most common examples of cardio include walking, cycling.

Body Building

It is distinct from similar activities such as powerlifting because it focuses on physical appearance instead of strength.

Boxing

Boxing is a combat sport in which two people, usually wearing protective gloves and other protective equipment such as hand wraps and mouthguards, throw punches at each other for a predetermined amount of time in a boxing ring.

Lifting

Weight training provides a stress to the muscles that causes them to adapt and get stronger, similar to the way aerobic conditioning strengthens your heart.
